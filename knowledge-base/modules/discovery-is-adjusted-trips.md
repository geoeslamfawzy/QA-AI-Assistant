- Adjusted trip is a system-generated record created to correct/reverse financial and status impact of a previously finished or cancelled trip
- Created when a cancelled trip is updated to Finished: new trip with **positive amount** (equal to original), status = `ADJUSTED`, budget is deducted
- Created when a finished trip is updated to Cancelled: new trip with **negative amount** (equal to original), status = `ADJUSTED`, budget is refunded
- `ADJUSTED` is a valid enum trip status
- Adjusted trip's `requestedAt` must match the original trip's date
- Adjusted trips are also created when a finished trip from a previous billing cycle is retroactively cancelled due to fraud
- Adjusted trips apply in B2B (enterprise) billing context for financial reconciliation
- Adjusted trips are invoiced separately via a dedicated "Facture d'Avoir" (credit note)
- A separate adjusted trips invoice is generated **only if** adjusted trips exist for the billing period; otherwise no adjusted invoice is generated
- Invoice header includes: adjusted date, `Avoir n°` (credit note reference), and reference to the corresponding finished trips invoice
- Both the original trip and its adjusted trip are hidden from fleet owner views
- Adjusted trips from fraud corrections must be completely hidden from drivers — not visible in Balance → Transactions or Ride History
- Adjusted trips are visible in WebApp/AdminPanel trip history and export files alongside other statuses
- [Unconfirmed] Adjusted trip is linked to original via a `relatedTo` field
- [Unconfirmed] Trigger timing may be immediate or batch-processed
